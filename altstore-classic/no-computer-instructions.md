# 📲 No Computer Instructions

{% hint style="warning" %}
This feature is still in beta and only available on Patreon. Instructions subject to change.
{% endhint %}

To use AltStore Classic without a computer, you will need to follow the below instructions.

### Set Up Steps

{% hint style="info" %}
Requires AltServer 1.8 or later.
{% endhint %}

1. Install [LocalDevVPN](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/localdevvpn/id6755608044) to your iOS device from the App Store
2. Configure remote AltServer
   1. Connect your iPhone or iPad to your Mac or PC via USB
   2. Go to Settings tab in AltStore Classic
   3. Tap "Configure Remote AltServer..." in the Remote AltServer section
   4. Tap "Continue" on the alert that appears
   5. Tap "Trust" on your iPhone or iPad and enter your passcode when prompted

### Instructions

1. Start VPN
   1. Launch LocalDevVPN on your iOS Device
   2. Tap "Connect"
2. Make sure you're connected to WiFi **(not cellular)**
3. **DONE!** You can now use AltStore to sideload and refresh your apps without connecting to AltServer on your computer.

{% hint style="info" %}
If you have issues, you can try picking a different remote AltServer by tapping "Choose Server" in AltStore's settings.
{% endhint %}
